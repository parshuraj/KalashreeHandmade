Our Provenance

The Legacy of Kalashree Handmade

Every piece at Kalashree Handmade is a testament to centuries of goldsmithing traditions passed down through generations. Our artisans hail from historic craft communities where the techniques of filigree, granulation, and stone-setting are treated as sacred arts.

We source our materials with the utmost respect for the earth and its people. Our gold is ethically refined, and our gemstones are hand-selected from trusted, conflict-free mines across the globe.

When you acquire a Kalashree creation, you are not merely purchasing jewelry; you are becoming the custodian of a story—a story of fire, hammer, time, and devotion.
